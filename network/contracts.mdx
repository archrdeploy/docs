## Deployment

Archr runs on Robinhood Chain mainnet (chainId 4663). Every deployed contract below is verified on Blockscout. The owner of each earlier launcher factory is the deploy wallet `0x9008C9eE6eF776c06D4aAFb4a4bfCF13083680c6`.

## Current launch stack

Every new launch, from the site, Reddit, or X, runs through the contracts in this section.

### `ArchrFactoryV3`

The launcher factory behind every new launch. Deploys [`Coin`](#coin) contracts, creates single-sided Uniswap V4 pools paired against native ETH at LP fee 0, and mints every launch-wall LP position directly to the burn address `0x000000000000000000000000000000000000dEaD`, so no address (owner included) can ever move it. On every launch it escrows 5% of the supply into [`TrancheEscrow`](#trancheescrow) in the same transaction and records the coin's creator; both are enforced by the contract. Public function surface:

- `collectCreatorFees(token)`: permissionless. Forwards the entire accrued vault balance to the recorded creator as native ETH. The vault is fed by [`ArchrHookV3`](#archrhookv3), which skims 1% of the ETH side of every trade. Enforced on-chain; no function exists to route it anywhere else. See [creator fees](/launch/creator-fees).
- `setCreator`: one-shot. Sets the creator for a coin that launched without one (Reddit and X launches, claimed via `!claim`). A set creator can never be changed, and every set is publicly evented.
- `stageTickerDisable` / `activateTickerDisable`: the [ticker registry](/launch/tickers). A staged disable can be activated by anyone 14 days after staging; the contract enforces the window, so the program cannot skip it, subject to the contract owner's publicly evented veto and emergency override (see [tickers](/launch/tickers)). Launching a disabled ticker reverts `TickerDisabled`.
- `launchMigration`: the entry point for [migrations](/launch/migrations). Migrations are self-serve and permissionless; one migration per source coin is enforced on-chain.

Airdrop launches through this factory also check the source coin's spot FDV on-chain and revert `SourceBelowFloor` under $50k. The spot check is a deterrent rather than manipulation-proof; the site's 48-hour median check, computed off-chain by the platform, is the quality gate. See [Airdrop Mode](/launch/airdrop-mode).

```
0x218B50eab2Cc0B8786c414f58F8e33bA9851F1FA
```

[View on Blockscout](https://robinhoodchain.blockscout.com/address/0x218B50eab2Cc0B8786c414f58F8e33bA9851F1FA)

### `ArchrHookV3`

The ownerless singleton Uniswap V4 hook attached to every new coin's pool. On every trade it skims a flat 1% of the ETH side (both buys and sells) into that coin's per-coin vault, which the [factory](#archrfactoryv3) later forwards to the recorded [creator](/launch/creator-fees) as ETH. The hook has no owner, no parameters, and no pause; it holds no ETH at rest.

```
0x1017120aE98B4a54452EbaAB12F4960d33dF60Cc
```

[View on Blockscout](https://robinhoodchain.blockscout.com/address/0x1017120aE98B4a54452EbaAB12F4960d33dF60Cc)

### `ArchrLaunchpadV3`

The public payable adapter for site launches, fronting [`ArchrFactoryV3`](#archrfactoryv3). The wallet that calls it is recorded as the coin's creator, enforced by the contract.

```
0xaF0B1b922d183d6D773b519C58d19b19A59C9D2D
```

[View on Blockscout](https://robinhoodchain.blockscout.com/address/0xaF0B1b922d183d6D773b519C58d19b19A59C9D2D)

### `TrancheEscrow`

Holds every new coin's escrow (5% of supply, 50M tokens; 2% on a [migration](/launch/migrations) launch) in a per-coin pot and pays out the [holder rewards](/earn/holder-rewards) tranches. Tranche amounts and unlock times are fixed at launch and enforced on-chain: an early or oversized release reverts, and the platform cannot withdraw escrowed funds during the program. Public function surface:

- `distributeTranche`: pays an unlocked tranche in batched on-chain transfers to the recipient list, which is computed off-chain by the platform from published snapshot blocks.
- `sweepExpired`: after a 30-day time gate from launch, enforced by the contract, lets the contract owner sweep only that coin's unspent leftovers (failed sends, dust, pots with no eligible recipients).

```
0xCF6a256e504a5a62717a2D4379894143B1083450
```

[View on Blockscout](https://robinhoodchain.blockscout.com/address/0xCF6a256e504a5a62717a2D4379894143B1083450)

## Earlier launchers

New launches use the [current launch stack](#current-launch-stack) above. Every factory below serves coins launched before the current program: each stays deployed to hold those coins' liquidity permanently, and none is used for new launches. Coins launched through them keep their original behavior, including the [legacy fee-flywheel](/earn/archr-flywheel).

### `ArchrFactoryV2`

The V3-pool generation of the launcher factory, used for launches before the move to [`ArchrFactoryV3`](#archrfactoryv3). Deploys [`Coin`](#coin) contracts, creates single-sided Uniswap V3 pools at fee tier 1% (`FEE = 10000`, `TICK_SPACING = 200`), and holds every LP NFT permanently locked. Its coins collect fees through `collectCreatorFees(token)`, paid to the recorded creator with the WETH leg unwrapped to ETH and the coin leg delivered as-is; they carry the 5% [holder-rewards](/earn/holder-rewards) escrow and the ticker registry.

```
0x4d94bC6894a2D0026E8F819C18e5e6d1286dA5dA
```

[View on Blockscout](https://robinhoodchain.blockscout.com/address/0x4d94bC6894a2D0026E8F819C18e5e6d1286dA5dA)

### `ArchrLaunchpadV2`

The payable adapter site launches used in front of [`ArchrFactoryV2`](#archrfactoryv2), before the move to [`ArchrLaunchpadV3`](#archrlaunchpadv3). The wallet that called it was recorded as the coin's creator, enforced by the contract.

### `ArchrFactory`

The consolidated launcher factory that served launches from the site and the Reddit and X bots before the current program. Deploys [`Coin`](#coin) contracts, creates single-sided V3 pools, and holds every LP NFT permanently locked, with the same `SUPPLY = 1e27`, `FEE = 10000`, `TICK_SPACING = 200` constants as the earlier factories. It adds [Airdrop Mode](/launch/airdrop-mode): a launch can reserve 30% or 60% of the supply (enforced on-chain) and send it to the [`AirdropDistributor`](#airdropdistributor) in the same transaction.

```
0xcCD6d3e586DE8f5644856303038025a97dCe68c1
```

[View on Blockscout](https://robinhoodchain.blockscout.com/address/0xcCD6d3e586DE8f5644856303038025a97dCe68c1)

### `AirdropDistributor`

Receives the reserved source-holder share of an [Airdrop Mode](/launch/airdrop-mode) launch and distributes it pro-rata to the source coin's eligible holders. Owned by the deploy wallet. It cannot mint or reprice anything; it only forwards the reserved balances it already holds to the snapshot recipients.

```
0x7a27d5E568Ed24Eb37DCf8b962d8b01F26b51833
```

[View on Blockscout](https://robinhoodchain.blockscout.com/address/0x7a27d5E568Ed24Eb37DCf8b962d8b01F26b51833)

### `ArchrLaunchpad`

The payable adapter the site trigger used before the current program; site launches now go through [`ArchrLaunchpadV3`](#archrlaunchpadv3). Stateless (no owner, no pause, no storage) and immutable in its wiring to [`ArchrFactory`](#archrfactory). Entry points: `launch(...)` and `launchWithBuy(...)` for plain launches, `launchWithBuyFromPost(...)` for launches keyed to an external post identifier, and `launchWithAirdrop(...)` / `launchWithAirdropAndBuy(...)` for [Airdrop Mode](/launch/airdrop-mode) launches.


### `Coin`

The token template every launcher factory deploys via CREATE2. See [FAQ → what does a coin look like on-chain](/reference/faq#what-does-a-coin-look-like-on-chain) for the properties. The `RedditFactory` and `TwitterFactory` deploy byte-identical renames (`RedditToken`).

Coin addresses are per-launch (deterministic from launcher + salt + name + symbol). See [launch overview](/launch/overview) for how a launch is triggered on-chain.

### `ArchrLauncher`

The one-shot launcher used to deploy the ARCHR platform token itself. Separate from the coin factories above. It launches a single coin, then `launched` flips to `true` and the contract cannot launch again. Locks 70% of ARCHR's supply as LP; the other 30% was minted to the deployer at launch.

## ARCHR

### ARCHR token

The Archr platform token: a plain ERC-20 with fixed supply `1,000,000,000e18`. See [FAQ](/reference/faq#what-does-a-coin-look-like-on-chain) for the properties. Holding ARCHR makes an address eligible for [holder rewards](/earn/holder-rewards) on every new launch and, on coins launched before the current program, the [legacy fee-flywheel](/earn/archr-flywheel).

```
0xce8F89b7d4AD8936531D764055E6b1a06e30Ab58
```

[View on Blockscout](https://robinhoodchain.blockscout.com/address/0xce8F89b7d4AD8936531D764055E6b1a06e30Ab58)

### ARCHR WETH V3 pool

The Uniswap V3 pool ARCHR trades on. 1% fee tier, tickSpacing 200. Locked-LP NFT held by [`ArchrLauncher`](#archrlauncher). **This pool address is excluded from [holder rewards](/earn/holder-rewards) snapshots and from the legacy flywheel's top-50 snapshot**: its ARCHR balance is unsold single-sided liquidity, not a real holder.

```
0x47d4DA247eA887248448aaA8528C556B2399bC31
```

[View on Blockscout](https://robinhoodchain.blockscout.com/address/0x47d4DA247eA887248448aaA8528C556B2399bC31)

### ARCHR fee-flywheel distributor

The address the [legacy fee-flywheel program](/earn/archr-flywheel) uses as its working balance for coins launched before the current program. Collected fees land here, the WETH-leg swap happens from here, and the top-50 airdrop is sent from here. Every airdrop shows up as 50 outgoing transfers of the coin-of-the-cycle.

```
0x5b82a4D03Fe13C45f9fF9A8d4eB394D35cb18b67
```

[View on Blockscout](https://robinhoodchain.blockscout.com/address/0x5b82a4D03Fe13C45f9fF9A8d4eB394D35cb18b67)
